WELCOME TO DeeShu

Redefining contemporary hospitality for guests, DeeShu is all about the modern conveniences, especially curated to make stay
experiences one-of-a-kind.

Located in Bangalore, the property is in close proximity to major landmarks of the city, including Kumarakrupa Road, Gandhinagar,
MG Road, Brigade Road, Vidhana Soudha, ISKCON Temple, Indiranagar, UB City and other known locations of this bustling
metropolitan.

People from all across the world visit the city for business and leisure purposes and DeeShu makes sure that while they’re here, guests
get to experience the unique “Y-Gen” lifestyle. With an aim to redefine conventional hospitality, DeeShu aims to offer an unforgettable
experience to the travellers.

Other attractions that are located a few minutes away from the property are Race Course, Chickpet and other such prominent
locations.

OUR
BRAND STORY

DeeShu is a premium hotel brand under Great Destinations Hotels & Resorts Pvt. Ltd. The name DeeShu is contemporary and brings to mind a mood and tone that defines our brand. If you are looking for something that is fresh, vivid and contemporary you have come to the right place. Conveniently located, with state-of-the-art facilities, our services aim to Energize, Engage and Entertain.
The first “Y-Gen” lifestyle hotel in the city, DeeShu aim to redefine the conventional hospitality experience with our contemporary, yet opulent offerings that offer all our guests something truly unique. Designed to be ultra modern with an infusion of indigenous design elements, DeeShu aims to offer an unforgettable experience to all that stay with us.
